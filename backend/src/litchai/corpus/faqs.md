# Litch Consulting — Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I request a service?

Sign in to the client dashboard or use the Get Started flow on the website,
choose the service you need, upload the requested documents, and submit. You will
receive a confirmation and, for fixed-price services, an invoice. Our team then
opens an engagement and begins work.

## How much do services cost?

Fixed-price services display their Naira price before you submit; the price shown
is VAT-exclusive and 7.5% VAT is added on the invoice. Bespoke services such as
forensic accounting, modelling and advisory are quoted after a short review of
your requirements.

## How do I pay an invoice?

Every invoice has a secure public pay link. Open the link, review the line items,
and pay online. A branded PDF receipt is issued automatically once payment
clears. You can also view all your invoices and receipts in the client dashboard.

## What is the turnaround time?

Turnaround depends on the service and the completeness of your documents.
Financial statements typically take 5 to 10 business days after documents are
received; tax filings depend on statutory deadlines; modelling and advisory are
scoped per engagement. Each service page shows its indicative turnaround.

## Which documents do I need to provide?

It depends on the service. For financial reporting we typically need your trial
balance or ledger, bank statements, and prior-year accounts. For tax work we need
the relevant computations, invoices and payroll records. Each request lists its
required documents before you submit.

## Is my data secure?

Yes. Documents are encrypted in transit and at rest. Financial documents sent to
our analysis pipeline are encrypted with a blind-relay envelope the moment they
arrive, and only ciphertext is forwarded — we log metadata only, never the
contents of a file. You can request erasure of your data at any time.

## Can I get help with an existing engagement?

Yes. Open a ticket from the Help Desk in your dashboard or reply to your
engagement email. Support requests are tracked and assigned to the team member
handling your work.

## Do you work with clients outside Lagos?

Yes. Litch works with clients across Nigeria and remotely. Documents are
exchanged securely online, and meetings can be held virtually.
