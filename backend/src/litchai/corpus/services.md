# Litch Consulting — Services Catalogue

Litch Consulting is a Nigerian professional finance firm. We serve businesses,
NGOs and individuals across Nigeria with reporting, modelling, tax, forensic and
advisory work. All engagements are delivered in Naira (NGN) and comply with the
Nigeria Tax Act 2025 and IFRS.

## Financial Reporting

We prepare IFRS-compliant financial statements — statement of financial position,
profit or loss, statement of cash flows, statement of changes in equity, and the
accompanying notes. We handle annual reports (IAS 1 and IFRS 18 presentation),
management accounts, and statements of affairs. Typical turnaround is 5 to 10
business days once source documents are complete. Required documents: trial
balance or general ledger, prior-year financials, bank statements, and any
schedules for fixed assets, loans and receivables.

## Financial Modelling

We build formula-driven financial models for budgeting, forecasting, valuation,
and fundraising. Models are delivered as auditable Excel workbooks with clearly
separated inputs, calculations and outputs, plus scenario and sensitivity
analysis. Common models: three-statement models, discounted cash flow (DCF)
valuations, project finance models, and loan amortisation schedules.

## Taxation

We handle Nigerian tax compliance and advisory end to end: Companies Income Tax
(CIT) and the Development Levy, Value Added Tax (VAT), Withholding Tax (WHT),
Pay As You Earn (PAYE), and personal income tax. Services include tax
computation, filing, FIRS and State IRS correspondence, tax health checks, and
planning. We apply the rates in the firm's versioned Nigeria tax configuration so
every computation is traceable to law.

## Forensic Accounting

We investigate suspected fraud, financial misstatement and asset
misappropriation. Engagements cover fund tracing, transaction reconstruction,
data analysis of ledgers and bank statements, expert reports, and litigation
support. Deliverables are evidence-backed reports suitable for management, audit
committees, or the courts.

## Data Analytics

We turn raw financial and operational data into decisions: dashboards, KPI
reporting, revenue and cost analysis, anomaly detection, and cash-flow analytics.
We work from bank statements, ERP exports, POS data and spreadsheets, and can
automate recurring reporting.

## Advisory

We advise on business strategy, financial controls, systems and process
improvement, company setup and CAC compliance, and transaction support. Advisory
is scoped per engagement and usually quoted after a discovery call.

## How to request a service

Clients request a service through the Litch website's Get Started flow or the
client dashboard. Each request captures the service, the required documents, and
the engagement period. Fixed-price services show the price up front (VAT
exclusive at 7.5%); bespoke work is quoted after review. Uploaded documents are
encrypted in transit and at rest.
